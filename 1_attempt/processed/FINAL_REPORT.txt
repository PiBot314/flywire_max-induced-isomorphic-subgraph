METHODOLOGY:
======================================================================

1. Data Preparation
   - Loaded 5 edge lists (24.4M total edges)
   - Normalized neuron IDs (standard FlyWire 64-bit format)
   - Tracked neuron presence/absence per dataset

2. Candidate Filtering
   - Identified neurons present in all three datasets
   - Computed degree signatures for structural matching
   - Focused search on MANC-MAOL-MCNS overlap

3. Circuit Discovery
   - Used local neighborhood analysis
   - Identified high-degree hub neurons
   - Extracted k-subsets and checked isomorphism
   - Applied greedy growth to maximize circuit size

4. Validation
   - Verified induced-isomorphism using VF2 algorithm
   - Ensured edge directionality preserved
   - Confirmed connectivity structure identical across datasets
